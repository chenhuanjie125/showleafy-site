Contact support

Email support@showleafy.com directly. You can describe the issue, share a suggestion, or explain what you need help with.

Usually replies in

Most emails are answered within 3-5 business days. Timing may be slightly delayed around releases, holidays, or heavy support periods.

You can email about these topics

  • App usage issues and bug reports
  • Feature requests and product feedback
  • Purchase, restore-purchase, or entitlement questions
  • Privacy and data-handling inquiries

Helpful details to include in your email

  • App version
  • iOS or iPadOS version
  • Device model
  • Steps leading up to the issue
  • Relevant screenshots, screen recordings, or logs
  • Order screenshots or order numbers if the issue is purchase-related

Developer information

Showleafy is currently operated independently by an individual developer. The developer name shown on the App Store will match the legal name registered with the Apple Developer account.
